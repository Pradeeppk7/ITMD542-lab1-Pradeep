About Us

The Product Management Club is a vibrant community at Illinois Tech dedicated to fostering innovation, leadership, and excellence in product management.

We bring together passionate students who are interested in understanding the intersection of technology, business, and user experience. Our mission is to equip members with the skills, knowledge, and network needed to excel in the product management field.

Through workshops, case studies, mentorship programs, and collaborative projects, we create an environment where future product leaders can learn, grow, and make an impact.
